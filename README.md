## Project 1: Fracture

### Creative Brief
Project Goal: Deliver a short high energy brand identity
Concept: A 10-second logo animation for a fictional brand. A solid polygon fractures into three wedge fragments that pull apart and drift, then reverse and converge back toward center, interlocking into a single complete polygon at the moment of impact becoming whole again. A glow and shockwave mark that moment of reassembly, then the brand name and tagline are revealed.
Assets Used:
•
fracture.ai (shockwave, wedge_a,wedge_b,wedge_c, text)
Design Decisions: I chose the fracture-and-reassemble concept because it gives the animation a clear payoff, three pieces that complete each other instead of just moving around on screen. Splitting the polygon into three fragments meant each piece needed its own position and rotation animation to sell the idea that they're drifting apart and then racing back together, and all three had to land perfectly in place on the same frame or the shape wouldn't read as whole again. The glowing, pulsing burst at the moment of reassembly reinforces that payoff instead of feeling like a separate effect layered on top. I also used a track matte wipe for the wordmark instead of a simple fade, so the reveal feels triggered by the impact.
Software Used:
•
Adobe Illustrator
•
Adobe After Effects
